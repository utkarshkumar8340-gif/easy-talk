About EasyTalk-Spoken

EasyTalk-Spoken is a personal English coaching institute run by Kundan Kumar in Jehanabad, Bihar. We help students, job-seekers, and working professionals build real spoken English confidence — not just bookish grammar.

Over 3+ years, we've trained 500+ students through small batches, daily speaking practice, and personal attention — earning a 5-star reputation in the local community.

Whether your goal is a better job, cracking an interview, or simply speaking fluently in daily life — we have a course built for you.
